Madrigal Houses

Artwork 1

Artist: Renz Rainier Pasagdan

The Casita itself is a vibrant and enchanting structure, adorned with colorful patterns and intricate designs. The magic within the house is a central element of the story, as it provides each family member with a special power that contributes to the overall harmony and well-being of the family and the community. The Madrigal house becomes a symbol of the family's unity and the strength that comes from embracing each member's distinctive qualities. The Casita is a magical place filled with love, warmth, and the rich cultural heritage of the Madrigal family.

The Man

Arwork 2

Artist: Sean Denver Smaniego

In this captivating artwork, the stark contrast between the weathered face of an adventurous wanderer, marked by the sun's embrace and lines etched with tales of exploration, converges with the polished countenance of an urban intellectual, radiating intellect and refinement. Through a single gaze, the painting encapsulates the duality of divergent lifestyles—one shaped by the raw allure of nature's unpredictability and the other by the nuanced complexities of urban existence. Together, these faces tell a visual tale of two distinct worlds harmonizing in the canvas of life.

Bird

Artwork 3

Artist: Jomar Dindo

Celebrating the Breathtaking Avian Wonders of the Philippines From the vibrant plumage of the Philippine Eagle to the melodious songs of the Luzon Bleeding-heart, our archipelago is a haven for unique and endangered bird species. Join us in marveling at the diverse feathered treasures that grace our skies, embodying the rich biodiversity and natural beauty of the Philippines.

The Beauty

Artwork 4

Artist: John Zenn Godani

Physical beauty may be reflected in features such as symmetry, clear skin, expressive eyes, and a radiant smile. However, true beauty often transcends these superficial aspects, encompassing a person's unique charm, kindness, and the way they carry themselves. Ultimately, beauty is a dynamic and personal experience, influenced by cultural, societal, and individual perspectives.

Checkmate

Artwork 5

Artist: John Earl Rance

"Checkmate" is a term commonly used in the game of chess when a player's king is in a position to be captured and there is no way to escape, resulting in the end of the game. In the context of love, the concept of "checkmate" can be metaphorically applied to signify a profound and decisive moment in a romantic relationship. It might represent a moment of clarity or realization when two individuals recognize and accept their mutual feelings, leading to a harmonious and conclusive connection. In this metaphorical sense, "checkmate" in love could symbolize the victory of mutual understanding, emotional alignment, and the endearing capture of each other's hearts, marking the triumph of love over uncertainties and hesitations.
